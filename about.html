Luiz Felipe Ribeiro

My name is Luiz Felipe Ribeiro, I'm a brazilian freelance and I've been working and studying graphic design for the last 10 years. I'm a graduated of Universidade Veiga de Almeida (UVA) and I have also studied a few years at Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio). I work with video editing, post production, visual effects, motion graphics, artwork, photo, 3D, logo development/visual identity and web design.

I have worked on many projects with different people and companies such as Observatório Nacional (DAED), PUC-Rio (CCESP), Channel OFF, YOPIGO, Massive Fire, Hatefulmurder, PIN UP Pole Studio, Andrade e Ribeiro, CERCRED, Luiza Scarpa, Kellys Kelfis, Isidoro Kutno and Eduardo Ponti. Please do not hesitate to contact me. I would love to help you with your project.
